## Overview
A data-driven analysis to improve approval rates and risk scoring for a BNPL product.

## Problem
The approval accuracy for BNPL customers was inconsistent and resulted in higher NPAs in certain segments.

## Approach
- Analyzed repayment patterns, product-usage and risk signals  
- Built segmentation logic  
- Identified key variables for underwriting  
- Designed dashboards and MIS  
- Collaborated with sales + credit teams

## Solution
Improved credit model features, added rule-based filters, and proposed automated workflows.

## Impact
- Improved approval accuracy
- Reduced manual intervention
- Faster TAT for onboarding
